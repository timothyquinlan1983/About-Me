Who is Tim Quinlan?

Tim Quinlan was born in Winter Haven, FL and spent his childhood growing up in both Miami, FL and Indianapolis, IN. Until COVID-19 put an abrupt end to his career, he was a professional DJ that worked gigs all across the Midwestern United States under the name WindyTimmy. He's currently working in the real estate industry and studying coding and web development at Northeastern Illinois University in Chicago.

Where is Tim Quinlan now?

Tim Quinlan now resides in Chicago, IL, but still visits Miami and Indianapolis regularly.
